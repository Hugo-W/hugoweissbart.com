SINES - AXNS Collective

an LSD brain wave and sound art hackathon done in 2017

Hugo Weissbart
Last updated on Dec 13, 2020 1 min read Art
Image credit: SINES

This piece was produced over a two-day LSD brain wave and sound art hackathon in March 2017, organised by AXNS Collective and in collaboration with Imperial College London and Music Hackspace.

Bringing sound artists and neuroscientist together to synthesize sonic atmospheres and music from MEG activity recorded by Dr. Robin Carhart-Harris, Head of Psychedelic Research in the Division of Brain Sciences at Imperial College London. Listen to more here.
